Our Closest Chapter - Komodo Island

Experience the Magic of Komodo Island

"Explore the captivating wonders of Komodo Island! Home to the iconic Komodo dragons, this tropical paradise offers breathtaking landscapes, pristine beaches, and vibrant underwater life. Immerse yourself in the unique blend of adventure and relaxation as you hike through rugged terrain, snorkel in crystal-clear waters, and witness stunning sunsets. Discover a world of natural beauty and intrigue on Komodo Island."

Activity List

Arrive at Komodo Airport, transfer to accommodation. Visit Komodo National Park Visitor Center. Hike to Padar Island viewpoint for stunning vistas. Relax and dine at your accommodation.

Early tour to witness Komodo dragons on Komodo Island. Snorkel at Pink Beach's coral-rich waters. Picnic lunch on a secluded beach. Explore Rinca Island, observe dragons, and enjoy nature.

Dive to discover diverse underwater life. Brunch, souvenir shopping, or beach relaxation. Join a local cooking class for Indonesian cuisine. Share memories over a farewell dinner.
